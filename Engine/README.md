# Requirements
* Greater than 250 hp
* Automotive diesel engine
* Turbocharged
* Liquid cooled
* Available in the US
* Less than $15,000 before modifications
* Weight less than 500 lbs if less than 300 hp
* Weight less than 700 lbs if greater than 300 hp
* Engine heater included
* Steel pistons
